AAA CONDUCTORS

AAA CONDUCTORS: AAAC stands for All Aluminum Alloy Conductor, and it's a type of overhead conductor used for power transmission and distribution. AAAC conductors are made from multiple layers of aluminum alloy that are stranded in concentric layers. They are designed to have a high strength-to-weight ratio, improved electrical properties, and superior corrosion resistance.

ACSR CUNDUCTORS

ACSR CUNDUCTORS: Aluminium conductor steel-reinforced cable (ACSR) is a type of high-capacity, high-strength stranded conductor typically used in overhead power lines. The outer strands are high-purity aluminium, chosen for its good conductivity, low weight, low cost, resistance to corrosion and decent mechanical stress resistance. The centre strand is steel for additional strength to help support the weight of the conductor. Steel is of higher strength than aluminium which allows for increased mechanical tension to be applied on the conductor. Steel also has lower elastic and inelastic deformation (permanent elongation) due to mechanical loading (e.g. wind and ice) as well as a lower coefficient of thermal expansion under current loading. These properties allow ACSR to sag significantly less than all-aluminium conductors.

Aerial Bunched Cables

Aerial Bunched Cables (ABC) offer a modern, safe, and efficient solution for overhead power distribution. Their compact design, safety features, and durability make them a preferred choice for both urban and rural installations. By combining practicality with aesthetics, ABC cables contribute to a more reliable and visually appealing electrical infrastructure, while minimizing risks associated with traditional overhead power lines. As urbanization increases and the need for reliable power distribution grows, ABC cables are poised to become an essential component of modern electrical grids.

LT Power cable

Our LT (Low Tension) power cables are designed to provide safe and efficient power distribution for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Rated for voltages up to 1kV, these cables ensure the reliable transportation of electricity while minimizing the risk of electrical hazards.

LT Power Armoured Cable

LT Power Armoured Cable (Low Tension Power Armoured Cable) is a type of electrical cable designed for the distribution of electrical power in various applications, typically under low voltage conditions (up to 1kV). These cables are used to ensure the safe and efficient transmission of electricity, especially in areas where mechanical protection is needed.
